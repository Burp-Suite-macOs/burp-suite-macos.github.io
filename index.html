Burp Suite for Mac

Burp Suite is the integrated platform for performing security testing of web applications. Its tools work seamlessly together, supporting the entire process—from initial mapping and analysis of an application's attack surface to finding and exploiting vulnerabilities.

Intuitive for beginners, highly configurable for experts. Start testing immediately with powerful, extensible features.

Intercepting Proxy

Inspect and modify traffic between your browser and the target application in real time. Full control over requests and responses.

Application-Aware Spider

Crawl content and functionality effortlessly. Map your application's attack surface with precision and speed.

Advanced Scanner

Automate detection of numerous vulnerability types. Comprehensive scanning powered by industry-leading intelligence.

Powerful Attack Tools

Intruder for customized attacks, Repeater for request manipulation, Sequencer for session token analysis. Exploit vulnerabilities efficiently.

Extensibility and Workflow

Save your work, resume anytime. Write custom plugins for complex tasks. Seamless integration across all tools.

How It Works

Step 1: Configure Proxy

Set up the intercepting Proxy in your browser. Traffic flows through Burp for inspection and modification.

Step 2: Map the Application

Use the Spider to crawl and discover content. Analyze the full attack surface automatically.

Step 3: Scan for Vulnerabilities

Launch the Scanner to detect issues. Review results with detailed reports and evidence.

Step 4: Exploit and Test

Employ Intruder, Repeater, and Sequencer. Customize attacks, manipulate requests, and verify fixes.
